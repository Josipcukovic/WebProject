ROGER TAYLOR

Roger Meddows Taylor OBE(born 26 July 1949) is an English musician, singer, songwriter, multi-instrumentalist best known as the drummer for the rock band Queen. As a drummer, Taylor was recognised early in his career for his unique sound. He was voted by radio listeners as the eighth-greatest drummer in classic rock music history in a poll conducted by Planet Rock in 2005.As a songwriter, Taylor contributed songs to Queen's albums from the beginning, composing at least one track on every album, and often singing lead vocals on his own compositions.He contributed a further five major hits ("Radio Ga Ga", "A Kind of Magic", "Heaven for Everyone", "Breakthru", and "The Invisible Man").

Some interesting facts

  • He loved their song 'It's a Hard Life' but he hated the video because he thought that he looked rather stupid in his costume.
  • Some of his inspirations include; Keith Moon of The Who, Mitch Mitchell of The Jimi Hendrix Experience and John Bonham of Led Zeppelin.
  • Always before a show he would take 2 shots of scotch,no more,no less.
  • He has collaborated with artists such as; Eric Clapton, Roger Waters,Robert Plant, Roger Daltrey, Phil Collins, Genesis, Jimmy Nail, Elton John, Foo Fighters, Bon Jovi.
